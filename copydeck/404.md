> **Notes:** The lead is fine — plain, non-dramatic, practical. The three CTAs cover the three most likely things a lost visitor actually wants: the homepage, the work, or to ask a question. The portfolio button correctly uses "See" — standing rule ("no view as a verb") is satisfied.
>
> **Standing rule reminder:** No "view" as a verb in buttons or links, anywhere on the site. Use see, browse, explore, find, read.

## COPY NOTE

The 404 page doesn't need to be clever. A wry one-liner might fit the voice — "Nothing here but a broken link and good intentions" — but it's also fine to leave it functional. Don't add one unless there's a specific reason; the current copy is unobtrusive. If you do want something more characterful, it should be dry rather than apologetic.
